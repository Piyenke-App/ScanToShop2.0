Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 12, 2026

ScanToShop 2.0 ("ScanToShop", "we", "us", or "our") is a barcode scanning and price comparison application built for the Pi Network. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the data needed to operate the app:

  • Pi Network account information: When you sign in through the Pi SDK, we receive your Pi username to attribute community-submitted prices. We do not collect or store your Pi wallet passphrase, private keys, or wallet password.
  • Location data (optional): If you grant permission, we use your device's approximate location to estimate distances to nearby stores. Location is processed on your device and is not stored on our servers.
  • Barcode and product lookups: When you scan or enter a barcode, the app queries the Open Food Facts public API to retrieve product names and images.
  • Community price submissions: Store names, prices, barcodes, and your Pi username associated with prices you submit are stored locally in your browser and may be shared with other users as part of the app's price comparison feature.
  • Camera access: Used only when you choose to scan a barcode. We do not record or store camera footage.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

  • Email addresses, phone numbers, or mailing addresses
  • Pi wallet passphrases, private keys, or seed phrases
  • Payment card or banking information
  • Precise location history

3. How We Use Information

  • Display product details and compare prices near you
  • Allow authenticated Pi users to contribute store prices
  • Improve app reliability and user experience

4. Third-Party Services

ScanToShop uses the following third-party services:

  • Pi Network SDK — authentication (minepi.com)
  • Open Food Facts API — product information (world.openfoodfacts.org)
  • Google Fonts — typography delivery (fonts.google.com)

These services have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them.

5. Data Storage and Retention

Price contributions you make are stored in your browser's local storage on your device. We do not operate a centralized database for user accounts beyond what Pi Network provides through authentication. You can clear locally stored data by clearing your browser storage for this app.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share your data with advertisers. Product barcode lookups are sent to Open Food Facts as described above. Pi authentication is handled by the Pi Network SDK.

7. Children's Privacy

ScanToShop is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information processed by the app. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. Never share your Pi wallet passphrase or private keys with any app, including ScanToShop.

9. Your Rights and Choices

  • You may decline location access and still use manual barcode entry.
  • You may choose not to sign in with Pi; browsing and scanning remain available, but price submission requires Pi authentication.
  • You may clear local app data through your browser settings.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect the latest revision. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact the app developer through your Pi App Studio listing or the support channel provided at publication.
